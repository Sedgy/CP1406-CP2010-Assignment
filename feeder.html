Benefits of Music

"One good thing about music, is when it hits you, you feel no pain."

This quote by Bob Marley suggests that music has mental and physical benefits. Scientists have been able to perform research that proves this to be true. Some benefits of music include:

  • Improve sleep quality
  • Reduce stress
  • Induce a meditative state
  • Relieve symptoms of depression
  • Elevate mood
  • Improve cognititve performance
  • Reduce anxiety
  • Increase creativity

If you want to improve your life come join us at Townsville Community Music Centre!
